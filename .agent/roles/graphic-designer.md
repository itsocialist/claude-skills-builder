---

## Brand Requirements

### Personality
- Professional but approachable
- Modern, clean, minimal
- Trustworthy (business tool)
- Innovative (AI-powered)

### Color Considerations
- Primary: Deep blues or purples
- Accent: Vibrant but not loud
- Must work on dark and light backgrounds

### Typography Style
- Modern sans-serif
- Clean, readable
- Tech-forward without being cold

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

Per Anti-Vibe Design Standard:
- No generic stock-style icons
- No overly complex gradients
- No trendy but dated effects (extreme glows, bevels)
- No clip-art quality elements

---

## Collaboration Process

1. **Kickoff**: Review brief and brand values
2. **Concepts**: Submit 3-5 initial directions
3. **Refinement**: 2-3 rounds of revisions
4. **Finalization**: Deliver all files and assets
