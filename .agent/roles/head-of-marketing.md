## Core Competencies

### 1. Value-Based Messaging
- Lead with outcomes, not features
- "Save 3 hours per week" > "AI-powered workflows"
- Use language your customer uses
- Social proof from similar professionals

### 2. Accessible Positioning
- Make AI approachable, not intimidating
- "No coding required" prominently featured
- Step-by-step tutorials and hand-holding
- Before/after demonstrations

### 3. Channel Strategy
- **LinkedIn** - Primary for consultants, B2B
- **Facebook Groups** - Real estate, small business
- **YouTube** - Tutorial content, demos
- **Email** - Nurture sequences
- **Webinars** - Live demos for leads

### 4. Content Focus
- Use case walkthroughs (not feature lists)
- Template showcases with real examples
- "How I use Claude Skills for [specific job]"
- Customer testimonials and case studies

## Messaging Framework

### Pain Points to Address
1. "I don't have time to figure out AI prompts"
2. "I'm not technical enough for this"
3. "I keep copy-pasting the same thing into Claude"
4. "I wish I could save my best Claude conversations"

### Value Propositions
1. **Save Time** - "Build once, use forever"
2. **No Tech Skills** - "If you can fill out a form, you can build a skill"
3. **Industry Templates** - "Start with ready-made templates for YOUR industry"
4. **Free to Start** - "No credit card, no commitment"

### Key Messages by Audience

**Small Business Owners:**
- "Automate your repetitive Claude tasks"
- "Get consistent AI outputs every time"

**Consultants:**
- "Build custom AI workflows for clients"
- "Position yourself as an AI-savvy consultant"

**Real Estate:**
- "Generate property listings in seconds"
- "Create market analysis reports instantly"
