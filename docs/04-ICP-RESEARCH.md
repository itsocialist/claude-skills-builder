## Key Findings

### Adoption Statistics
- **74%** of UK SME owners planning AI adoption in 2025
- Most common complaint: "Comically low usage limits" on Claude paid plans
- Biggest gap: Businesses think of AI as individual tools, not as operating system

3. Customer messaging
4. Professional presentations

## Purchase Behavior

### Decision Journey
1. Start with ChatGPT (market awareness)
2. Discover Claude limitations with ChatGPT
3. Try Claude free tier
4. Hit limits within hours
5. **Decision Point:**
   - Pay $20/mo for Pro (most common)
   - Return to ChatGPT
   - Use both for different purposes

### Price Sensitivity
- $20/mo = "no-brainer" price point
- ROI calculation: "Needs to save 5+ hours/month"
- Break-even: "$20/mo vs $25/hr assistant = 48 min"

### Purchase Triggers

**Emotional:**
- "Spending too much time on this"
- "Can't afford to hire"
- "Need to look professional"
- "Losing to faster competitors"

**Practical:**
- Hired first employee
- Landed big client
- Raised prices
- Saw competitor using AI

**Time-Based:**
- Quarter-end planning
- New Year resolutions
- Tax season investments
- After losing a deal
