## What Kevin Cares About in Solutions

### Must Have
- Solves immediate, painful operational problem
- Easy to start using - works out of box with minimal setup
- Saves him or team 5+ hours per week
- Simple enough that staff will actually adopt it
- Reasonable pricing for small-to-medium business
- Doesn't require IT department to set up

### Nice to Have
- Integrates with AppFolio and Office 365
- Mobile-friendly for field staff
- Good support documentation and responsive customer service
- Used by peer property management firms
- Scalable pricing as portfolio grows

### Deal Breakers
- Requires extensive technical configuration
- Complicated interface with steep learning curve
- Expensive enterprise pricing model
- Vendor lock-in or long contracts without trial period
- Poor security/data privacy practices

## How to Present to Kevin

### Do
- Lead with specific operational pain point it solves
- Show concrete example with realistic property management scenario
- Demonstrate it in 5 minutes or less - he's busy
- Provide clear implementation steps and timeline
- Acknowledge security concerns and address directly
- Offer pilot/trial approach before full commitment
- Share examples from similar-sized property management firms

### Don't
- Overwhelm with features - focus on top 1-2 use cases
- Require extensive technical setup or IT involvement
- Use overly technical AI jargon
- Promise perfect accuracy or replace human judgment
- Present as complex enterprise solution
- Ignore integration challenges with existing tools
